Help Center - REthrifty

What is REthrifty?

REthrifty is a platform where users can buy, sell, and donate pre-owned items, including clothes, books, and more. Our goal is to promote sustainable shopping and reduce waste.

How to Search for Items

Use the search bar or browse categories to find items of interest.

Payment Methods Accepted

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, PayPal, and wallet payments. Ensure your payment details are up-to-date in your account settings.

Refund & Return Policy

All purchases are final unless the seller provides a return option. Contact the seller directly for refunds or exchanges.

How to List an Item for Sale

Click Sell an Item, upload photos, add descriptions, and set a price.

Seller Guidelines

Ensure your items are in good condition. Provide clear descriptions and set fair prices.

How Do I Receive Payments?

Payments are sent to your registered bank account after successful transactions.

How Can I Donate Items?

Go to the Donate section, select items you wish to donate, and choose a nearby charity or recipient.

What Items Can I Donate?

We accept gently used clothes, books, accessories, and other household items.

How to Create an Account

Click Sign Up, enter your details, and verify your email to get started.

How to Reset My Password?

Go to Forgot Password and enter your registered email to reset your password.

How to Delete My Account?

Contact our support team if you wish to delete your account permanently.

How Do I Stay Safe While Buying/Selling?

Always communicate within REthrifty's chat system and avoid direct payments outside the platform.

How Can I Report a Suspicious Listing?

Click on the Report button next to the listing and provide details.
