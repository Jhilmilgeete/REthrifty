About Rethrifty

We are a community-driven platform promoting sustainability through donations and recycling. Our mission is to help individuals and families by providing them with the opportunity to donate unwanted items and support environmental causes.

What We Accept

Old Clothes

Gently used clothing that can be repurposed or recycled.

Books

Unwanted books, textbooks, or novels that can be given a second life.

Toys

Gently used toys that can be donated to children in need.

Household Items

Various household items that are still in good condition.

Our Process

Collection

We collect items from donors through our platform.

Verification

Items are verified for quality and condition.

Distribution

Verified items are distributed to those in need.
